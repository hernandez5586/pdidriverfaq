Single Trip Delivery:

When you need to deliver one trip this is the process you will use

  1. At the "Trip List" screen, quick tap on the trip that needs to be delivered.

  2. Once you have quickly tapped on the trip, you will be taken to the "Delivery Screen"

  3. At the "Delivery Screen" input the following:
    1. "POD Name": Type in nurses name
    2. Choose "Delivery Status" from drop down menu
    3. Tap on "Signature" and have nurse sign
    4. Once you have all three inputs selected/filled. Tap on "Finished". This will take you back to the "Trip List" screen and you have completed delivery of this trip. ** The "Finished" button will not be highlighted until you have selected all three inputs above. **

Multiple Trip Delivery:

When you need to deliver multiple trips this is the process you will use:

  1. At the "Trip List" screen, long press any trip going to the facility you are delivering to.

  2. After long pressing on a trip, you will come to a screen where you will need to select the nursing stations you will be delivering too. You may do one of the following on this screen:
    • 2a. Select 1 nursing station to deliver.
    • 2b. Select more than 1 nursing station to deliver.
    • 2c. Select All nursing stations to deliver.

  3. Next at the "Delivery Confirmation" screen, you will need to long press all trips being delivered until they disappear. Once all trips have disappeared you can press the next button.

  4. Next will be the delivery screen. Here you will need to collect the following before delivering a trip:
    • 4a. Type nurses name in "POD Name" field
    • 4b. Select "Delivery Status"
    • 4c. Get nurses signature
    • 4d. Tap "Finished" to complete delivery

Deliver Trips:

Below are the delivery instructions for the new version of IDS (v529):

  1. At the "Trip List" screen, scan a trip being delivered to the home you are at.

  2. After scanning a trip, you will come to a screen where you will need to select the nursing stations you will be delivering too. You may do one of the following on this screen:
    • 2a. Select 1 nursing station to deliver.
    • 2b. Select more than 1 nursing station to deliver.
    • 2c. Select All nursing stations to deliver.

  3. Next at the "Delivery Confirmation" screen, you will need to scan all trips being delivered to that nursing station. Until they disappear. Once all trips have disappeared you can press the next button.

  4. Next will be the delivery screen. Here you will need to collect the following before delivering a trip:
    • 4a. Type nurses name in "POD Name" field
    • 4b. Select "Delivery Status"
    • 4c. Get nurses signature
    • 4d. Tap "Finished" to complete delivery
